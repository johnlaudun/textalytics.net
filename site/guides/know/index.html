Good to Know

There are some things that any adult, especially a learned adult, needs to know in our current era.

  • If you don’t know about Open Culture, then you should take a look. A variety of materials – audio books, textbooks – are available there. In particular, I regularly use their archive of films for teaching.
  • Kenneth Burke’s “Literature as Equipment for Living” is too useful not to be available more broadly: URL.
  • The “The Zipf Mystery”.
  • A Short History and Explanation of GREP.
  • MICHEL FOUCAULT: An In-depth Explanation in Clear Language is a brief and easy to understand explanation of the thought of French thinker Michel Foucault, one of the most cited figures in the social sciences.
